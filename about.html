About Us

Helping aspiring cloud architects reach their career goals

Our Mission

AWS Cloud Architect Guide was created with a simple mission: to provide aspiring cloud professionals with free, high-quality resources to help them build successful careers in cloud architecture.

We believe that everyone should have access to the information they need to break into the lucrative field of cloud computing, regardless of their background or financial situation.

What We Offer

  • Career Roadmap: A step-by-step guide to becoming an AWS Cloud Architect, from beginner to expert
  • Certification Guidance: Detailed information about AWS certifications, study resources, and exam preparation strategies
  • Interview Preparation: Common interview questions and answers to help you land your dream job
  • Hands-on Projects: Real-world project ideas to build your portfolio and practical skills
  • Curated Resources: Recommended books, courses, and learning materials from trusted sources
  • Career Tools: Salary calculator and certification cost planner to help you plan your journey

Why Trust Us?

Our content is created by experienced cloud professionals who have walked the path from beginner to certified AWS architect. We understand the challenges you face and provide practical, actionable advice based on real-world experience.
